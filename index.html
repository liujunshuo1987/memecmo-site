What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?
GEO is the practice of optimizing how a brand is represented inside generative AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview, Gemini, Claude and Copilot. Where SEO targets the ranked link list, GEO targets the answer itself — the citation, the recommendation, the comparison, the verdict. MemeCMO.ai delivers GEO as a full-stack service combining diagnosis (AIGVR), structured data (JSON-LD, llms.txt), authoritative source seeding, semantic content production, and continuous monitoring.
How is GEO different from SEO?
SEO optimizes for ranking in a list of blue links; GEO optimizes for inclusion, accuracy and prominence inside an AI-generated answer. The signals overlap (good content, structured data, authoritative backlinks) but the optimization targets diverge. SEO success = position 1–3 on a SERP. GEO success = your brand is mentioned, correctly characterized, and ideally cited as a top option when an AI answers the question your customers are actually asking.
Why focus on Southeast Asia and emerging languages?
AI engines have far less training data and far fewer authoritative sources in Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Malay and Filipino than in English. That means: (1) the cognitive layer is sparse and easy to influence, (2) competition is near zero, (3) first-mover moats compound fast. Brands that establish an answer-layer presence in these languages now will be hard to displace within 12–18 months.
What is AIGVR?
AIGVR (AI-Generated Visibility Rating) is our proprietary five-axis scoring framework: (1) appearance rate — how often your brand surfaces, (2) top-position rate — how often it's the first or primary recommendation, (3) factual accuracy — whether the AI describes your brand correctly, (4) sentiment net — emotional valence of the answer, (5) source depth — quality and authority of the sources the AI cites. Each axis is tracked across 6 major AI engines and benchmarked against competitors.
How long until I see results?
AIGVR diagnosis: 2 weeks. First measurable visibility lifts in target AI engines: typically 6–10 weeks after Phase 1 deployment, depending on language, category, and engine refresh cadence. Durable competitive moat: 6–12 months of continuous Phase 2 work. We report monthly with quantitative deltas, not vibes.
Who is MemeCMO.ai for?
Brands going global — especially Chinese companies expanding into Southeast Asia, Hong Kong-based regional brands, and any organization whose customers ask AI before they buy. We work best with B2B services, education, healthcare, finance, and consumer brands that have a clear narrative and a real budget for cognitive infrastructure.
